Continuing the Legacy ...

Cambridge School Model United Nations was born from the inception of designing a platform to cater to the limitless potential of a community of students. Since the humble beginnings of our establishment in 2010, we have come a long way, hosting over a score of conferences for our young and passionate delegates while bestowing upon them a unique opportunity to take up leadership roles and navigate the intricacies of global politics. We aim to honor our founding principles while adapting to changing times. By learning from our experiences, we can maintain a lasting legacy that inspires the future generations to carry the torch of pride and dedication.
